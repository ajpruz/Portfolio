About Alexis

Hello! I'm Alexis Portillo, a passionate 29-year-old industrial engineer who found his true calling in the world of programming and web development. Since I began my journey in front-end development in 2020, I have explored and mastered multiple platforms and technologies to provide quick and effective solutions.
My career took an exciting turn when I discovered WordPress and other CMS, which allowed me to streamline and optimize the web development process. Over time, I have expanded my skills and knowledge in crucial areas such as Project Management, using efficient tools like Notion and Excel, as well as advanced website development with Webflow.

Continuous improvement and constant learning are pillars in my professional life. I have specialized in SEO positioning techniques on Google, including the use of tools like Google Analytics and Google Search Console, to ensure that every project I handle not only meets but exceeds expectations. Additionally, I manage projects with Jira, which allows me to maintain an organized and efficient focus at every stage of development.

Personally, I consider myself a responsible and friendly person, always seeking new challenges that allow me to grow and push my limits. My proactive attitude and constant desire to learn new skills drive me to give my best in everything I do. I am convinced that every challenge is an opportunity to demonstrate my ability and commitment.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a little better. I am excited about the opportunities and challenges that the future holds, and I am ready to face them with enthusiasm and dedication.
